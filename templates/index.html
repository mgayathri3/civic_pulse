How CivicPulse Works

Our platform streamlines the process of reporting and resolving community issues through a simple, transparent workflow.

1. Report an Issue

Submit details about your issue including location, photos, and description. Our AI helps categorize and route it to the right department.

2. Track Progress

Follow your report's journey through the system with real-time status updates and estimated completion timelines.

3. Resolution

Receive confirmation when your issue is resolved and provide feedback on the process to help improve civic services.
